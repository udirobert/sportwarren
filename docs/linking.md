# WhatsApp Linking Flow

SportWarren users can link their WhatsApp number to their account to unlock the **Kite Agentic Economy** features (Scout, Hire, Pay, Status).

## 🚀 How to Link

### 1. Start on Telegram
1. Open the [SportWarren Telegram Bot](https://t.me/sportwarrenbot).
2. Send the command `/linkwhatsapp`.
3. The bot will reply with a unique 6-character code (e.g., `WA-3F9A1C`).

### 2. Verify on WhatsApp
1. Message our WhatsApp number: **+1 (201) 534-5384**.
2. Send the message `link WA-3F9A1C` (replacing with your actual code).
3. Marcus (Academy Director) will confirm your account is linked.

---

## 🛰️ Why Link?

Linking connects your WhatsApp number to your **PlayerTwin** and **Squad Manager** agents on the Kite Chain.

| Command | Unlinked | Linked |
|---------|----------|--------|
| `help` | ✅ | ✅ |
| `find <query>` | ✅ | ✅ |
| `status` | ❌ | ✅ (Your squad analytics) |
| `scout <opponent>` | ❌ | ✅ (USDC settlement on Kite) |
| `hire <agentId>` | ❌ | ✅ (Reputation-gated delegation) |
| `pay <wallet> <usdc>`| ❌ | ✅ (Direct USDC wages) |

---

## 🛠️ Security & Privacy
- Codes expire after **15 minutes**.
- Only one WhatsApp number can be linked to a SportWarren account at a time.
- We never share your phone number; it is stored securely as a platform identity hash.
